Volleyball

volleyball is a 6 person team game. players have to hit the ball in the air over a net that is 7ft 11in. a point is scored when the ball touches the ground on one side of the court. each side can only hit the ball 3 times before having to hit the ball over the net.

Tips and Tricks

How to serve

When doing an overhead serve. You want to throw the ball at a good height. With your non-dominant hand you are going to aim to where the ball is and with your dominant hand you are going to bring your elbow back and bring it high and swing with an open palm and hit the ball.

How to Bump

Being able to bump pass is fundemental this is the start of any play. the better you can pass the better the team can succeed

How to Set

Setting is important to get the ball to a specific part of the cort. the better you can set the better you can support your team
